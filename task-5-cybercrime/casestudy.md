# TASK 5 – CYBERCRIME AWARENESS
## CYBERCRIME CASE STUDY
## Topic: Phishing Attack
Phishing is a type of cybercrime where attackers trick individuals into revealing
sensitive information such as passwords, bank details, or OTPs. This is usually done
through fake emails, messages, or websites that appear to be legitimate.
In a typical phishing attack, a user receives an email that looks like it is from a
trusted organization, such as a bank. The email asks the user to click on a link and
update their account details. The link redirects to a fake website that looks identical
to the real one. When the user enters their details, the attacker captures the
information.
Students and working professionals are common targets because they frequently
use online services. The consequences can include financial loss, identity theft, and
unauthorized access to accounts.
Phishing attacks are increasing due to lack of awareness and careless online
behaviour. Therefore, it is important to verify sources and avoid clicking on
suspicious links.
